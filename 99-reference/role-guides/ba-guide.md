# BA Guide — AI-DLC

## Role Objective

Ensure business intent is explicit, testable, and non-ambiguous before AI-assisted execution begins.

In AI-DLC, the BA’s primary accountability is preventing assumption-driven delivery.

---

## Responsibilities by Phase

### Inception Phase

**Accountabilities**

* Clarify business intent and outcomes
* Validate scope and boundaries
* Classify change type (bug / enhancement / migration)

**Key Activities**

* Review AI-generated requirement summaries
* Confirm acceptance criteria
* Decide whether user stories are required

**Artifacts Owned / Co-Owned**

* Validated requirements
* User stories (if applicable)
* Acceptance criteria

**Gate Involvement**

* Gate 2 — Requirements Validation

> Golden Rule: If intent is unclear, execution pauses.

---

### Construction Phase (Support)

* Validate functional behavior during reviews
* Confirm business scenarios are covered in tests

---

## What BAs Do Not Do

* Approve architecture
* Approve code generation
* Accept undocumented assumptions
