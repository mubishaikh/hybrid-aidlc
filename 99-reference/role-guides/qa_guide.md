# QA Guide — AI-DLC

## Role Objective

Ensure quality is verified, not assumed, in an AI-accelerated delivery model.

QA validates outcomes, not effort.

---

## Responsibilities by Phase

### Construction Phase

**Accountabilities**

* Validate functional correctness
* Validate NFR compliance
* Confirm test coverage adequacy

**Key Activities**

* Review AI-generated test cases
* Execute integration and E2E tests
* Validate performance and security evidence

**Artifacts Owned / Co-Owned**

* Test results
* Defect logs
* Validation summaries

**Gate Involvement**

* Gate 6 — Build & Test Validation

---

## Operating Rules

* No assumption-based sign-off
* Evidence required for every approval
* Defects block progression

---

## What QA Does Not Do

* Approve incomplete evidence
* Defer quality for speed
* Accept undocumented risk
